# Protocol: Entropy Compressor (Logarithms)

## 1. Directive
**"Contain the Infinite."**

Unchecked exponential growth ($2^x, 10^x$) creates **Entropy Runaway**, rapidly exceeding system memory and processing limits. The **Entropy Compressor** (Logarithmic Scale) is the only safeguard against this singularity. It maps vast magnitudes into manageable linear space.

## 2. The Metaphor
- **Exponential Growth ($x \to \infty$)**: A "wildfire" or "explosion". It consumes all available space on the graph/screen instantly.
- **Logarithm ($\log x$)**: The "containment field" or "compressor". It crushes the explosion down to a flat line.
- **Base ($b$)**: The "compression ratio". $\log_{10}$ suppresses faster than $\log_2$.

## 3. Mission Architecture

### Phase 1: Signal Runaway (The Danger)
*Objective: Witness the failure of linear observation.*
- **Action**: Increase Signal Intensity ($x$) linearly.
- **Observation**: The Exponential Curve ($2^x$) shoots off-screen almost immediately ($x > 10$).
- **Status**: **CRITICAL OVERLOAD**. The system cannot render the magnitude.

### Phase 2: Compression Lock (The Solution)
*Objective: Engage the Logarithmic Scale.*
- **Action**: Activate `COMPRESSION_MODE` ($\log_{10}(y)$).
- **Observation**: The vertical axis transforms. $1,000,000$ becomes $6$. The exponential curve becomes linear.
- **Result**: **SIGNAL STABILIZED**. We can now visualize $x=50$ ($2^{50} \approx 10^{15}$) without crashing.

### Phase 3: The Decibel Scale (Real World)
*Objective: Calibrate sensitivity.*
- **Context**: Human senses (hearing, earthquake perception) operate logarithmically.
- **Action**: Map "Earthquake Magnitude" (Richter) to Energy Release.
- **Lore**: "The Operator perceives the universe not in raw energy, but in Orders of Magnitude."

## 4. System Terminology
- **Input**: `SIGNAL_INTENSITY` ($x$)
- **Output**: `ENTROPY_LEVEL` ($y$)
- **Mode**: `LINEAR` (Unsafe) vs `LOGARITHMIC` (Safe)
- **Status**: `NOMINAL` | `OVERLOAD` | `CONTAINED`

## 5. Visual Identity
- **Palette**: Deep Red (Danger/Exp) -> Cool Cyan (Safe/Log).
- **Motif**: "Crushing" vertical lines. Scanlines.
- **Feedback**: Screen shake or "glitch" artifacts when in Linear Mode with high $x$.
